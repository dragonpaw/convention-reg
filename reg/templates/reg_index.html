Instructions

Registering a member

In order to register a member, you must first create a member record for that user which will be used for this, and all future conventions. To do so, click on 'Add Person' on the left.

Once a member has a record, you can register them for this event. If they attend a future event, you will not need to recreate the member record as it will carry over from year-to-year and event-to-event.

Checking pre-reg, or adding to an existing member.

Find the membership from the 'Person List' on the left, and then click on their name to see a list of all memberships they have purchased. If they would like to buy another membership, you may do so from that screen.
